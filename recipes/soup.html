Pumpkin Orange Banana Smoothie

Smooth and creamy, this unconventional smoothie is as delicious as it is nutritious. Its deep orange colour is a clear sign this smoothie is brimming with antioxidants—it's packed with beta carotene and vitamin C.

Ingridients:

  • 1 cup / 250 ml skim milk, or unsweetened pea milk, or unsweetened soy milk
  • 1/2 cup / 125 ml pure pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup /125 ml 100% orange juice
  • 1 medium banana, sliced
  • 4 ice cubes, optional

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and purée until smooth. Serve cold.
